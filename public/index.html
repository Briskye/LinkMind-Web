Jan 16, 2020

Philippine Offshore Development Services Launched

Linkmind Apps and Testing Inc., Angeles, Philippines, since January 2020 we have begun accepting offshore software development services.

The cost per person-month is about 20～30% lower than the cost in Japan. In principle, the service is in English, but a bridge SE (interpreter and engineer leader)

can be added to the development system (similar to the cost per person-month in Japan), so contract development in Japanese is possible.

We have a wealth of young talent in the Philippines, so we will strengthen our recruitment efforts in the future and further enhance our system.
